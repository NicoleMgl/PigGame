Instructions: Pig Game

X

The goal of the game is to get to 50 points first.

Players go one at a time ROLL a die. If a player rolls a 1, they forfeit all accumulated points and it is the other player's turn.

Players can HOLD their score in order to keep their rolls from being forfeitted. When you HOLD your score, the next player gets to go.
